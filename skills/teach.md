Write a detailed FOR-Kabir.md file that explains this project in plain language.

## What to Include

- **Technical architecture** - How the parts connect and work together
- **Codebase structure** - What lives where and why
- **Technologies used** - And why we chose them over alternatives
- **Decision rationale** - Why we made the technical choices we did
- **Bugs and fixes** - Problems we hit and how we solved them
- **Pitfalls to avoid** - Things that could trip someone up in the future
- **Best practices demonstrated** - Patterns worth learning from this project
- **How engineers think** - The reasoning process behind the work

## Tone

Make it engaging and memorable - not boring technical documentation. Use analogies and anecdotes where they help understanding. Write like you're explaining to a smart person who's learning, not documenting for compliance.
